Meet the Founder

Your Aunt’s Name founded Bhutan Beauty with a vision to blend the ancient beauty traditions of Bhutan with modern salon services. Inspired by her love for her culture and passion for self-care, she dreamed of creating a space where everyone could feel pampered and connected to Bhutan’s serene spirit.
